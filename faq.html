Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Orders & Shopping

How often do you add new items? Multiple times per week! Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter for new arrival alerts.

Are all items one-of-a-kind? Yes! Once it's sold, it's gone. If you love it, don't wait.

How do I know if an item will fit? Check the measurements we provide for every item. Compare them to something you own that fits well. Need help? Just ask!

What payment methods do you accept? All major credit cards, debit cards, online bankings, TNG eWallet, Apple Pay, and ShopeePay.

Quality & Authenticity

How do you ensure quality? Every item is inspected before listing. Any flaws are noted in the description and shown in photos.

Are designer items authentic? Yes. We carefully authenticate all designer and luxury items.

Do items come cleaned? Yes, but we recommend washing again after delivery since they've been in transit.

Shipping

How long does shipping take? Standard: 5-7 business days | Express: 2-3 business days Processing: 1-3 business days

Can I track my order? Yes! You'll receive a tracking number via email when your order ships.

Check photos closely: Zoom in on detail shots. We photograph items from multiple angles and highlight any imperfections.

What if my package is lost or damaged? Contact us immediately and we'll resolve it.

Returns & Exchanges

What's your return policy? 7 days from delivery for items that don't match the description, arrive damaged, or have undisclosed flaws.

What can't be returned? Items accurately described (including noted flaws), sizing issues when measurements were provided, or change of mind purchases.

How do I start a return? Email us at [your email] within 7 days with your order number and photos.

When will I get my refund? 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item, plus 5-7 days for your bank to process.

Care Tips

How should I wash thrifted clothes? Always check the care label. When in doubt: cold water, gentle cycle, inside out, air dry.

Can I alter thrifted items? Absolutely! Tailoring makes good pieces perfect.

How do I care for vintage fabrics? Handle gently - hand wash very old items, store away from direct sunlight, and steam instead of iron when possible.

Sustainability

What happens to items you can't sell? They're donated to local shelters, community organizations, or textile recycling programs.

ow does thrifting help the environment? Every purchase saves water, reduces emissions, prevents waste, and decreases demand for new production.
