Events

Join The Movement

We're more than just a thrift store - we're a community of people who believe fashion can be sustainable, accessible, and fun. Join us at our events, connect with fellow thrifters, and be part of something bigger.

Upcoming Events

What they are: In-person shopping experiences where you can see, touch, and try on curated thrifted pieces before anyone else.

What to expect:

  • First access to new inventory
  • Special event-only pricing
  • Styling advice from our team
  • A fun, relaxed shopping atmosphere
  • Meet other sustainable fashion lovers

Stay updated: Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter for pop-up announcements. These events sell out fast!

Collaborations

Local Artisan Partnerships:

We team up with local designers, upcyclers, and makers to give thrifted pieces new life. From hand-embroidered vintage jackets to custom-dyed denim, these collaborations create unique, one-of-a-kind items you won't find anywhere else.

Interested in collaborating? We're always looking for creative partners. Contact us with your ideas!

Brand Partnerships:

We work with sustainable fashion brands and eco-conscious businesses that share our values. These partnerships bring you exclusive collections, special discounts, and joint events.

Community Involvement

Donation Programs:

Give back while you clean out: We accept clothing donations year-round. Items we can't sell in our store go directly to local shelters and community organizations.

Drop-off info:TS4A, Kampung Batu 22,Jalan Yan 06900, Yan Kedah.

Drop-off date and hours:Every Wednesday from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.

We accept clean, gently used clothing, shoes, and accessories. The donations will be given the next day at local shelters and community organizations.

Local Charity Support:

A portion of our proceeds supports local environmental and social justice organizations. When you shop with us, you're contributing to:

  • Environmental conservation efforts
  • Community support programs
  • Youth education initiatives
  • Textile recycling programs

Sustainable fashion isn't just about what we wear - it's about how we come together to create change. Every event, collaboration, and connection strengthens our community and our impact.
