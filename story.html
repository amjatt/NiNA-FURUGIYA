Our Story

Our Journey

Nina Furugiya started as a small passion project, discovering unique and sustainable fashion pieces. Over time, it grew into a curated platform celebrating creativity, individuality, and thrift culture. Our vision is simple: provide modern fashion enthusiasts with access to stylish, sustainable, and story-rich clothing while building a community that values conscious choices.

The heart of our brand lies in our incredible team. Muhammad Amzar, our Founder & Creative Director, drives the overall vision and creative direction, ensuring every piece we showcase reflects uniqueness and quality. Naurah Qistina, as Operations Manager, manages the day-to-day logistics, making sure every order, collaboration, and project runs smoothly.

On the marketing side, Nor Muhammad Arif, our Marketing & Community Lead, connects with our audience through social media, community events, and campaigns, building strong engagement and loyalty. Behind the collections, Muhammad Zulkhairi, our Curation Specialist, carefully selects each thrift item, balancing style, quality, and sustainability. Last but not least, Amira Natasha, our Designer Specialist, adds creativity to styling and presentation, ensuring every outfit inspires and delights.

Together, this dedicated team has built Nina Furugiya into a brand that is more than fashion, it is a story, a movement, and a community of people who value style, sustainability, and individuality.
